Be Yourself Game Studios

Slime Climb

Climb to freedom as Hopper the happy slime by using his stretchy arms to grapple to walls! I worked with a team of other aspiring game developers to produce this game in the first week of starting university, for the Staffordshire University GT Jam! We called ourselves MicroPixel Studios. After all our hard work, we won the jam so be sure to check out our game below!

  • Play Here

Blaze

The spaceship shoot-em-up where you charge into your enemies head-first to smash them into oblivion! With Blaze, I wanted to flip the idea of a first person shooter on its head. Rather than avoiding enemies, the only way to destroy them is to fly straight into them! You still have tail missiles to protect yourself from enemy projectiles, so theres a great back and forth feel to the gameplay.

  • Play Here

Face the Swarm

An epic arcade-style top-down shooter! Survive the endless horde of undead using a wide array of weaponry! This was one of the first games I was able to finish and features multiple waves of zombies, weapon pickups and fast-paced action-packed gameplay!
