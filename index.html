Climb to freedom as Hopper the happy slime by using his stretchy arms to grapple to walls! I worked with a team of other aspiring game developers to produce this game in the first week of starting university, for the Staffordshire Uni GT Jam! We called ourselves MicroPixel Studios. Blaze

The spaceship shoot-em-up where you charge into your enemies head-first to smash them into oblivion! With Blaze, I wanted to flip the idea of a first person shooter on its head. Rather than avoiding enemies, the only way to destroy them is to fly straight into them! You still have tail missiles to protect yourself from enemy projectiles, so theres a great back and forth feel to the gameplay.
